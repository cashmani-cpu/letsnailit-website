1. Bookings and reservations

All reservations are subject to availability and are confirmed only upon receipt of payment or an explicit written confirmation from Learnovate Skills Private Limited (trading as Let's Nail It). Bookings made via the website form or WhatsApp are treated as reservation requests until confirmed.

2. Cancellations and refunds

Cancellations made more than 72 hours before the event date are eligible for a full refund or a date transfer. Cancellations within 72 hours of the event are non-refundable but may, at our discretion, be transferred to a future date. No-shows forfeit their ticket entirely. Refunds (where applicable) will be processed within 7–10 working days to the original payment method.

3. Event format

We operate an immersive murder mystery dining experience. All guests are assigned a role and are expected to participate in good faith. We reserve the right, without refund, to remove any guest who disrupts the experience for others, behaves aggressively, or is intoxicated to the extent that it impacts the evening for other guests.

4. Food and dietary requirements

We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, Jain and gluten-free requirements when notified at the time of booking. Please inform us of any food allergies or intolerances in advance. We cannot guarantee an entirely allergen-free kitchen environment and cannot be held responsible for allergic reactions where requirements were not disclosed before the event.

5. Private events and corporate bookings

Private event quotes are valid for 7 days from the date of issue. A deposit of 50% is required to confirm a private booking. The remaining balance is due no later than 7 days before the event date. Custom plots, scripts and supporting materials developed for a private event remain the intellectual property of Learnovate Skills Private Limited unless otherwise agreed in writing.

6. Photography and content

We may photograph or film parts of our events for marketing purposes. By attending, you consent to being incidentally photographed. If you would prefer not to be included in any promotional imagery, please notify us before the event begins and we will accommodate the request.
