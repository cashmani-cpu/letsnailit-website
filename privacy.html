Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  •  Learnovate Skills Pvt Ltd (Let's Nail It), Aga Abbas Ali Road, Bangalore  •  DPDP Act 2023 compliant

1. Who we are

Learnovate Skills Private Limited ("we", "us", "our"), trading as Let's Nail It, is a murder mystery supper club incorporated in India, with its principal place of business on Aga Abbas Ali Road, Bangalore, Karnataka. We operate the website at letsnailit.com. For any data-related enquiry, contact us at hello@letsnailit.com.

2. What data we collect

When you submit a reservation or private-event enquiry via our website form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your preferred event date (and, for private events, approximate guest count and event type)
  • Any additional notes you choose to share

We do not collect payment-card details through this website.

3. Why we collect it

We use the information you provide solely to: process your booking or enquiry, contact you to confirm your reservation, collect dietary or access requirements by direct conversation after you enquire, and notify you of changes to your booking. We do not use your data for profiling or automated decision-making, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. How we store and process your data

Form submissions are stored directly in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Let's Nail It. Google Sheets is provided by Google LLC and processes data under Google's published data-processing terms. No submission data is forwarded to any third-party email service.

5. Your rights under the DPDP Act 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you, correct inaccurate data, request erasure of your data (where we have no overriding legal obligation to retain it), nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of incapacity, and withdraw your consent for processing at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@letsnailit.com. We will respond within a reasonable period and in any event within the timelines specified by the DPDP Act 2023.

6. Data retention

We retain booking information for up to 2 years for accounting and legal purposes. Enquiries that do not result in a booking are deleted within 6 months. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

7. Cookies and tracking

This website does not currently use cookies for tracking or third-party analytics. No advertising pixels are embedded. Fonts are served by Google Fonts and Font Awesome is served by cdnjs; these requests may leave a temporary log entry with those providers. If we add analytics in future, this policy will be updated and consent (where required) will be obtained.

8. Grievance Officer

In line with the DPDP Act 2023, any grievance regarding the processing of your personal data may be addressed to our Grievance Officer at hello@letsnailit.com. You also have the right to make a complaint to the Data Protection Board of India.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related question or request, email hello@letsnailit.com.
