Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Learnovate Skills Private Limited (trading as Let's Nail It), Aga Abbas Ali Road, Bangalore  ·  Compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act)

1. Who We Are

Learnovate Skills Private Limited ("we", "us", "our"), trading as Let's Nail It, is a murder mystery supper club and events company incorporated in India, with its principal place of business in Bangalore, Karnataka. We operate the website at letsnailit.com. For data-related enquiries, contact us at hello@letsnailit.com or +91 97310 00440.

2. What Data We Collect

When you submit our reservation or private event enquiry form, we collect: your name, phone number, email address, dietary requirements, and any additional information you choose to provide. We do not collect payment card details through this website.

3. Why We Collect It

We use the information you provide solely to: process your booking or enquiry, contact you to confirm your reservation, and notify you of changes to your booking. We do not use your data for profiling or automated decision-making.

4. How We Store & Process Your Data

Form submissions are processed via Formspree (formspree.io), a US-based form handling service. Formspree is GDPR-compliant and processes data on our behalf under a data processing agreement. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. WhatsApp Communication

When you contact us via WhatsApp, your messages are processed by Meta Platforms (WhatsApp Inc.) in accordance with WhatsApp's own privacy policy. We use WhatsApp only to respond to enquiries and confirm bookings.

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you, correct inaccurate data, request erasure of your data (where we have no legal obligation to retain it), and withdraw consent for processing at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@letsnailit.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain booking information for up to 2 years for accounting and legal purposes. Enquiries that did not result in a booking are deleted after 6 months. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

8. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics at this time. If we add analytics in future, this policy will be updated.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at hello@letsnailit.com or WhatsApp +91 97310 00440.
