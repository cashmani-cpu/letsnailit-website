Why Let's Nail It?

What Exactly Is a Murder Mystery Supper Club?

Think of it as a dinner party meets detective game. You arrive, you're assigned a character with secrets, and over the course of a multi-course meal, a murder mystery unfolds. You interrogate fellow guests, bluff, form alliances, and try to figure out who the killer is - all while enjoying great food and drinks.

Where Is It Held?

Our supper club runs from our private dining space on MG Road, Bangalore. It's an intimate, curated setting - not a restaurant, not a bar. Think of it as being invited to the most dangerous dinner party in town. For private events, we come to your venue anywhere in Bangalore.

Do I Need to Act or Perform?

Not at all. You don't need to memorise lines or put on a show. You'll receive a character card with your backstory, secrets, and goals. How you play it is entirely up to you - whether you're a quiet strategist or a loud accuser, the game works either way.

Can I Come Alone or Do I Need a Group?

Both work. The supper club is designed for individuals, couples, and small groups to join a shared table of strangers. It's one of the best ways to meet new people in Bangalore. For private events, you bring your own crew of 8-40 guests.

Is Food Included?

Yes - always. Every supper club ticket includes a full multi-course dinner, welcome cocktails, and drinks throughout the evening. For private events, we offer food packages or you can arrange your own catering.

What Makes This Different From a Murder Mystery Kit?

Everything. We don't hand you a box of cards and wish you luck. Let's Nail It features original storylines, real game mechanics with scoring and strategy, and a fully designed experience from the moment you walk in. No awkward pauses, no confused hosts - just a seamless, high-energy evening where every single person at the table is part of the game.

What Is a Murder Mystery Dinner? (New to the Concept?)

A murder mystery dinner is a live-action interactive experience where guests play characters in an unfolding crime story — all over a full multi-course meal. Originally popular in the UK and US, murder mystery dinners are now emerging in India, and Bangalore (Bengaluru) is at the forefront.
